AtmosBeats – Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how AtmosBeats (“the App”) handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to respecting your privacy and keeping the data we handle to the minimum necessary to provide the service.

Data controller: Gürhan Kuraş

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location data

AtmosBeats uses your location only to determine the local weather for your area and generate music recommendations that match the current weather conditions.

  • Location access is requested through the iOS system dialog.
  • The App uses your location (for example, your city) to query a weather service.
  • Location data is not used to track you across apps or websites and is not sold to third parties.

1.2 Usage information

The App may collect basic, non-personal usage information such as which screens are viewed or which features are used. This is only used to improve the App and understand how it performs.

1.3 Music preferences

Within the App you can select music genres and favourite artists. These preferences are used only to generate better music recommendations for you. They are not shared with advertisers.

1.4 No account required

AtmosBeats does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address or other contact details inside the App unless you contact us directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide weather-based music recommendations tailored to you.
  • Display relevant information such as location name and weather.
  • Improve the design, performance and stability of the App.

3. Third-Party Services

AtmosBeats integrates with a small number of third-party services to deliver its core functionality:

3.1 Weather provider

We use a weather API to retrieve current weather conditions based on your approximate location. The request may include your city, coordinates or region so that the correct weather data can be returned.

3.2 Spotify

AtmosBeats uses the Spotify service to search for music, fetch album artwork and open tracks in the Spotify app. When you choose to open a track, you are redirected to the official Spotify application or website. Your use of Spotify is governed by Spotify’s own Terms and Privacy Policy.

3.3 AI-based recommendation engine

The App may send anonymised prompts (for example, weather and genre information) to an AI service to generate music suggestions. We avoid including personal identifiers in these prompts.

These third-party services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies separately.

4. Data Storage and Retention

We keep data only for as long as necessary to provide the App’s functionality and as required by applicable law. Weather information and generated recommendations are typically short-lived and not stored permanently with your identity.

5. Children’s Privacy

AtmosBeats is not directed to children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can delete it.

6. Your Choices

  • You can disable location access at any time in your iOS device settings. The App may then use a default location or may not be able to provide weather-based recommendations.
  • You can uninstall the App at any time to stop all data collection by AtmosBeats on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example when we add new features or integrate new services. When we do so, we will change the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after such changes means you accept the updated policy.
